The Strategy
Form, Structure & Sense

Match the parts. Get the points.

The strat

  1. Read the whole sentence — don't just look at the blank
  2. Find the subject and what the blank's job is
  3. Match what's around it — tense, number, form
  4. Eliminate anything that breaks the match

The quick checks

Plural subject?
Plural verb. "Objects WERE designed."
Present-tense passage?
Present-tense verb. Match what's around the blank.
Explaining WHY?
"To + verb" (infinitive of purpose).
Sentence opens with "-ing" phrase?
The next word must be the doer of that action.
"It's" vs "its"?
"It's" = "it is." "Its" = belongs to it.

Trap answers

  • Singular verb after plural subject (or vice versa)
  • Past-tense verb in a present-tense passage
  • "It's" used as a possessive
  • Dangling modifier — wrong noun right after the comma
