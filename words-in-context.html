Words in Context.

Vocabulary in passage context. Cover the choices, predict, then test. Watch for common words with secondary meanings.

The Strategy
Words in Context

The word that fits this exact sentence.

The strat

  1. Read around it — sentence with the word, sentence before, sometimes after
  2. Predict your own word before looking at choices
  3. Plug in each remaining choice to test fit
  4. Pick the most precise match

Context types to spot

definitionsynonymcontrasttone

Connotations matter

Same dictionary lane, opposite vibes. "Promising" and "ominous" both mean future-related, but tone tells you which fits.

Trap answers

  • Sound sophisticated but match dictionary, not context
  • The word's most COMMON meaning (CB usually picks the secondary meaning)
  • Too vague to be precise
